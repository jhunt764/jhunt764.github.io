Bio

My name is Jacob Patrick Hunt, hailing from outside of Charlotte, NC. I got involved in some javaScript coding classes in 2018 and since have fallen in love with just about anything involving code. I began my journey with a bootcamp prep course from flatIronSchool and later became a student at Bethel School of Technology. Bethel Tech features a one of a kind leadership element throughout the 9 month intensive and is powered by none other than the great Steve Wozniak's WozU. I have worked on numerous projects the most recent being a global hackathon focused on helping the immunocompromised during Covid-19. I also had the privilege of designing the website for Changed Choices, a non-profit organization located in Charlotte, NC that focuses on helping women who are incarcerated lead new lives beyond their time in prison. I have focus my training on ReactJS and Java, and I am eager to step further into the world of development.
